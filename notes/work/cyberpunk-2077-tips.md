## Build Tips

- **Netrunner build**: max Intelligence, grab Ping + Contagion early
- **Mantis blades**: pair with Reflexes 20 and Sandevistan for lethal melee
- Cyberpunk rewards exploration — check every alley in Night City

## Hidden Gems

- Judy's quest line is the best in the game
- Kerry's arc is surprisingly emotional
- Don't skip the Peralez quests
