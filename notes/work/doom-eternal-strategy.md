## Core Loop

- **Glory kill** to restore health, **chainsaw** for ammo, **flame belch** for armor
- Never stand still — constant movement is survival
- Prioritize Arachnotrons first to disable turrets

## Weapon Priority

- Ballista + Rocket Launcher for heavies
- Plasma Rifle to strip shields
- Super Shotgun with Meat Hook for fast repositioning
